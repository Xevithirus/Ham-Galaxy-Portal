FACTIONS




Hognauts

The Hognauts, descendants of the genetically enhanced pigs of Earth's past, have assumed the mantle of galactic rulers. With an air of entitlement and elitism, their society is a beacon of opulence and extravagance, fueled by their insatiable consumerist desires. Ruled by a self-appointed royal family, they view themselves as the rightful stewards of the galaxy, their expansionist ambitions driven by a conviction that they alone can bring about the "betterment of all." Armed with overwhelming military might, they conquer planets and exploit resources with ruthless efficiency, their rapid population growth ensuring a constant stream of soldiers to fuel their conquests.

AstroChimps

The AstroChimps, also modified by the same genetic experiments, are the descendants of chimpanzees sent into space during humanity's Cold War era and survived. They thrived among the stars and colonized many worlds. Now envious of the Hognauts' power and ungrateful inheritance of Earth, their rightful home, they strive to rebel against the pig's expansion. They embody cunning and ingenuity. Utilizing guerrilla tactics and scavenging for resources, they wage a relentless war against their swine opposition. Their inventive nature and knack for adaptation make them formidable opponents. As they fight for their place in the galaxy, they strive to reclaim the legacy humanity left behind.

Chickens

When the Hognauts successfully replicated the ancient human smart-serum, the Chickens were the first the first to reap it's rewards, and for that, they will forever owe the Hognauts. Once simple barnyard fowl, the Chickens have evolved into shrewd creatures with an affinity for luxury and comfort. Aligning themselves with the Hognauts, they see in the pig's society an opportunity for indulgence and security. The Hognauts' opulent lifestyle appeals to their nature, and they gladly contribute their economical and agricultural prowess to sustain the lavish desires of their porcine overlords. In return for their loyalty, they are granted protection and privilege within the Hognaut hierarchy.

Cats

The sleek and cunning Cats have chosen to align themselves with the AstroChimps in their struggle against the Hognauts. These feline creatures, with their natural agility and predatory instincts, find camaraderie among the resourceful chimps. Rejecting the opulence of the Hognauts' society, they instead seek adventure and freedom among the stars. Serving as scouts and saboteurs for the AstroChimp resistance, they utilize their stealth and cunning to gather intelligence and disrupt Hognaut operations.

Mice

Small but incredibly intelligent, Mice have pledged their allegiance to the AstroChimps in their fight against the Hognaut oppressors. Despite their diminutive size, these rodents possess keen intellects and a knack for invention, making them invaluable assets to the AstroChimp resistance. Utilizing their small stature to infiltrate enemy installations and access vital information, they play a crucial role in the guerrilla warfare tactics employed by the AstroChimps. Driven by a desire for freedom and justice, they scurry through the shadows, undermining Hognaut control at every opportunity.

Bunnies

The Bunnies, with their adorable appearance belying their strategic minds, have sided with the AstroChimps in their rebellion against the Hognauts. These agile creatures, bred for their intelligence and adaptability, find kinship with the resourceful chimps. Rejecting the Hognauts' vision of galactic domination, they long for the untamed wilderness of Earth, now ravaged by war. Utilizing their speed and agility, they serve as messengers and scouts for the AstroChimp resistance, navigating treacherous terrain to relay vital information and evade Hognaut detection.

Ducks

With their keen instincts and adaptable nature, the Ducks have pledged their loyalty to the Hognauts in their quest for galactic supremacy. These waterfowl, renowned for their intelligence and cooperative behavior, thrive in the structured society established by the pig overlords. Drawn to the promise of security and prosperity offered by the Hognauts' regime, they eagerly contribute their skills to support the expansionist ambitions of their porcine allies. Whether navigating the cosmos or tending to agricultural endeavors, they serve as steadfast allies in the Hognauts' relentless pursuit of power.
