HOGNAUTS

Pigs

The Hognauts, descendants of the genetically enhanced pigs of Earth's past, have assumed the mantle of galactic rulers. With an air of entitlement and elitism, their society is a beacon of opulence and extravagance, fueled by their insatiable consumerist desires. Ruled by a self-appointed royal family, they view themselves as the rightful stewards of the galaxy, their expansionist ambitions driven by a conviction that they alone can bring about the "betterment of all." Armed with overwhelming military might, they conquer planets and exploit resources with ruthless efficiency, their rapid population growth ensuring a constant stream of soldiers to fuel their conquests.

Ducks

With their keen instincts and adaptable nature, the Ducks have pledged their loyalty to the Hognauts in their quest for galactic supremacy. These waterfowl, though not the smartest bunch, value cooperative behavior, thrive in the structured society established by the pig overlords. Drawn to the promise of security and prosperity offered by the Hognauts' regime, they eagerly contribute their skills to support the expansionist ambitions of their allies. Whether navigating the cosmos or tending to economic endeavors, they serve as steadfast support in the Hognauts' relentless pursuit of power.

Whether patrolling their masters' opulent estates or sniffing out AstroChimp insurgents, these canine warriors remain steadfast in their commitment to the Hognaut cause. In return for their service, they are rewarded with affection and protection, their status elevated within the ranks of Hognaut society.

Chickens

When the Hognauts successfully replicated the ancient human smart-serum, the Chickens were the first the first to reap it's rewards, and for that, they will forever owe the Hognauts. Once simple barnyard fowl, the Chickens have evolved into shrewd creatures with an affinity for luxury and comfort. Aligning themselves with the Hognauts, they see in the pig's society an opportunity for indulgence and security. The Hognauts' opulent lifestyle appeals to their nature, and they gladly contribute their economical and agricultural prowess to sustain the lavish desires of their porcine overlords. In return for their loyalty, they are granted protection and privilege within the Hognaut hierarchy.

Sheep

Bred for obedience and loyalty, Sheep have found a comfortable existence among the Hognauts, embracing the relaxed lifestyle in uncontested zones. These woolly creatures, with their gentle disposition and cooperative nature, thrive in the structured society established by the pigs. Content to socialize in the lusher pastures of Hognaut-controlled worlds, they contribute to and support the extravagant desires of the pigs with no complaints. In return for their servitude, they are granted protection and care within the sprawling estates of the Hognaut elite, their loyalty unwavering in the face of AstroChimp resistance.

ASTROCHIMPS

Chimps

The AstroChimps, also modified by the same genetic experiments, are the descendants of chimpanzees sent into space during humanity's Cold War era and survived. They thrived among the stars and colonized many worlds. Now envious of the Hognauts' power and ungrateful inheritance of Earth, their rightful home, they strive to rebel against the pig's expansion. They embody cunning and ingenuity. Utilizing guerrilla tactics and scavenging for resources, they wage a relentless war against their swine opposition. Their inventive nature and knack for adaptation make them formidable opponents. As they fight for their place in the galaxy, they strive to reclaim the legacy humanity left behind.

Cows

These gentle giants, have joined the ranks of the AstroChimp resistance in their struggle against the Hognauts. Cows, with their docile demeanor, have long felt under appreciated and taken advantage of within the Hognaut societies. Rejecting the class based stucture of the pigs culture, they hold no value on status, and yearn for their own autonomy. Utilizing their strength and resilience, they provide vital support to the AstroChimp resistance, serving as both asset protection and powerful warriors in the fight to reclaim Earth. In solidarity with their fellow sentient beings, they stand against the tyranny of the Hognauts, determined to secure a better future for their kind.
